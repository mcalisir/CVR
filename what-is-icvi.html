What is I-CVI?

Item-level Content Validity Index: expert agreement ratio, formulas, and interpretation

I-CVI (Item-level Content Validity Index) measures expert agreement about whether a specific item on a measurement instrument is relevant and should be retained. It represents the proportion of experts who rate an item as essential.

I-CVI Formula

I-CVI = Number rating item as essential / Total number of experts

Acceptable I-CVI Thresholds

ExpertsCriterionStatus
1-2I-CVI = 1.0Valid ✓
3+I-CVI ≥ 0.78Valid ✓
3+0.70 to 0.78Needs revision
3+I-CVI < 0.70Remove item
