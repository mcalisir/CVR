What is S-CVI/UA?

Scale-level CVI Universal Agreement: strict consensus-based validity measure

S-CVI/UA (Scale-level Content Validity Index/Universal Agreement) measures the proportion of items on which all experts unanimously agreed the item is essential. It is a stricter criterion than S-CVI/Ave.

S-CVI/UA Formula

S-CVI/UA = Items with universal agreement / Total items

Validity Threshold

S-CVI/UA ≥ 0.80: Excellent validity ✓

At least 80% of items have 100% expert agreement.

S-CVI/Ave vs S-CVI/UA

  • S-CVI/Ave: Average agreement per item (more lenient)
  • S-CVI/UA: Unanimous agreement count (more stringent)
  • Both ≥ 0.80 together indicates excellent validity

References

Davis, L. L. (1992). Instrument review: Getting the most from a panel of experts. Applied Nursing Research, 5(4), 194-197.
